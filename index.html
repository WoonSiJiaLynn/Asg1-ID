About Blackpink

Meaning of the Name

The name BLACKPINK symbolizes “pretty isn’t everything.” It represents the blend of fierce (black) and sweet (pink) concepts.

Learn the History of How the Band Was Created!

2016 - Formation & Debut

BLACKPINK was formed by YG Entertainment and officially debuted on August 8, 2016 with the single album “Square One”:

Whistle — their first music show win.

Boombayah

They quickly became known for their powerful girl-crush concept, tight choreography, and strong visuals.

2017 - Rising Global Attention

They released “As If It’s Your Last”, which exploded internationally and became one of their signature hits.

As If It's Your Last

During this period, BLACKPINK started gaining huge global traction on platforms like YouTube and Instagram, breaking multiple records.

2018 - True International Breakthrough

They released the EP “Square Up” with the hit “Ddu-Du Ddu-Du”, one of K-pop’s most iconic songs.

Ddu-Du Ddu-Du

They also collaborated with Dua Lipa on “Kiss and Make Up.”

Kiss and Make Up

2019 - Coachella & Worldwide Fame

BLACKPINK became the K-pop girl group to perform at Coachella, gaining massive attention for their live performance.

They released “Kill This Love”, which further boosted their global recognition.

Kill this love

2020 - Record-Breaking Comeback

They released:

How You Like That

Ice Cream

Their first full album THE ALBUM, including “Lovesick Girls"

TThis era cemented them as the biggest girl group in the world.

2022 - Born Pink Era

The group released their second full album Born Pink, including:

Pink Venom

Shut Down

They held the Born Pink World Tour, one of the largest global tours for a girl group.

2023-2024 - Solo Activities

Each member expanded with solo work, acting, fashion, and dance projects.

2025–Present - DEADLINE

Jump

In 2025, BLACKPINK made a highly anticipated full-group comeback during the launch of their Deadline World Tour. On July 5 at the first concert in Goyang, they debuted a brand-new song called “Jump”, marking their first group release in nearly three years since Born Pink. Their comeback is tied to the tour, and they are reportedly working on a full album — their first major group record since 2022. All the best to the beloved group!
